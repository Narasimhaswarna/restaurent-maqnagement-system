Our history

Tradition, served daily

Grilli Restaurant, located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, is renowned for its delicious and flavorful cuisine that keeps food lovers coming back for more. Established with a passion for great taste and quality, it has become a beloved culinary destination in the city. Known for its rich flavors and exceptional service, Grilli Restaurant continues to delight guests with every dish served.

Special Dish

Ragi Sangati with Natu Kodi Pulusu

A wholesome and traditional Andhra delicacy, Ragi Sangati is made with finger millet and rice, rich in nutrition. Paired perfectly with spicy and flavorful Natu Kodi Pulusu (country chicken curry), this dish brings home-style comfort to your plate. A must-try for those who crave authentic, hearty village flavors
